what are birds of prey?

Birds of prey are also called raptors. They hunt other animals for food. They have big eyes, sharp claws (talons) and hooked beaks. Some examples are eagles, hawks, falcons, and owls.

Raptors are super important because they eat rodents and keep nature balanced. Without them, there would be too many mice and rats! That's kinda gross lol.
