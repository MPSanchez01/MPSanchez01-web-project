How they hunt

Eagles snatch fish right out of the water with their huge talons. Falcons do this crazy dive called a "stoop" and can go over 200 mph! Owls have special feathers that make them silent so mice don't hear them coming. Vultures just glide around looking for dead animals - not as exciting but still important.

Sadly, many raptors are in danger because of habitat loss and poison. DDT almost killed bald eagles but they banned it and now they're doing better.
